Netflix

Netflix is a subscription-based streaming service that lets you watch movies and tv shows for a monthly price.

80% of what people watch on Netflix is driven by recommendations.

Netflix was originally, temporarily named Kibble.

The name was meant to remind the founders that no matter how good the advertising was, the product had to be something people actually wanted.
