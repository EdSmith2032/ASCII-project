Wednesday

Wednesday Addams is expelled from her school after dumping live piranhas into the school's pool in retaliation for the boys' water polo team bullying her brother Pugsley. Consequently, her parents Gomez and Morticia Addams transfer her to their high school alma mater Nevermore Academy, a private school for monstrous outcasts, in the town of Jericho, Vermont. Wednesday's cold, emotionless personality and her defiant nature make it difficult for her to connect with her schoolmates and cause her to run afoul of the school's principal Larissa Weems. However, she discovers she has inherited her mother's psychic abilities, which allow her to solve a local murder mystery.

Stranger Things

Stranger Things is a science fiction horror series set in the 1980s in the small town of Hawkins, Indiana. The story begins with the mysterious disappearance of a young boy named Will Byers, prompting a desperate search by his friends, family, and the local police. Their investigation uncovers a secret government lab conducting experiments that open a gateway to an alternate dimension known as the Upside Down—a dark, dangerous mirror of the real world. The arrival of a strange girl with telekinetic powers, known only as Eleven, deepens the mystery and reveals that supernatural forces are at play. As the series progresses, the children and adults of Hawkins must confront terrifying creatures, secret experiments, and the growing threat from the other side—all while navigating the complexities of friendship, adolescence, and loyalty.

The Night Agent

The Night Agent is a gripping political action-thriller series that follows Peter Sutherland, a low-level FBI agent stationed in the basement of the White House, where he mans a rarely used emergency phone line for covert operatives. His mundane assignment takes a dramatic turn when he receives a distress call from Rose Larkin, a former tech CEO whose aunt and uncle have been assassinated. This call propels Peter into a complex conspiracy involving a mole at the highest levels of the U.S. government. As Peter and Rose delve deeper, they uncover a plot that threatens national security, forcing them to navigate a dangerous web of political intrigue, assassins, and betrayal. The series combines intense action sequences with a suspenseful narrative, exploring themes of trust, duty, and the moral complexities faced by those in positions of power. Based on the novel by Matthew Quirk, The Night Agent delivers a fast-paced and compelling story that keeps viewers on the edge of their seats.
